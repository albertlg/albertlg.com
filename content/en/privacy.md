## Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

- **Access:** know what personal data is being processed about you.
- **Rectification:** correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **Erasure:** request deletion of your data.
- **Restriction:** request that processing be restricted.
- **Objection:** object to processing based on legitimate interest.
- **Portability:** receive your data in a structured, portable format.

To exercise these rights, write to albert@albertlg.com. If you believe processing infringes data protection law, you may lodge a complaint with the [Spanish Data Protection Agency (AEPD)](https://www.aepd.es) or the supervisory authority in your country of residence.
