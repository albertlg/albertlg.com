I had read this some time ago, but I feel like bringing it up again:

During a group dynamics exercise for a multinational company, three candidates were asked the following question:
What would you like people to say about you at your funeral?
The first candidate said:
  - That I was a great doctor and a very good family man.
The second candidate said:
  - That I was a wonderful man, an excellent family man, and a teacher who had a great influence on the future of young people.
But the third candidate stole the show:
  - I’d like them to say... Look, he’s moving!!
That’s optimism... He got the job!
