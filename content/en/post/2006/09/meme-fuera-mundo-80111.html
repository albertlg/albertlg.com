This meme reached me via Ruth. Here’s my minute:

If it were the end of the world...

1. What song would you like to listen to?

“Black” by Pearl Jam... what I’m not sure about is whether I’d rather listen to it or sing it (I did the first yesterday at the Badalona concert, and I’ll do the second tonight at BeGood in Poblenou). ;)
2. What book would you like to leaf through?

“Typescript of the Second Origin” by Manuel de Pedrolo—I guess the topic has inspired me.
3. Who would you like to talk to?

Someone I love, for sure. But... about what? I don’t know, I don’t know... I think I’d rather have kisses and hugs.
4. What would you like to eat?

Three cannelloni: one made by Laura, another by my mother-in-law, and another by my mamma!
5. What would you do—what do you still have pending?

Forgetting that the world is ending, the first thing that came to mind was having children; although, if there’s going to be no world... The second would be writing a book; with no one to read it? Well, if it ends, it ends, right?
