How could it not have been a trip? We’re talking about a children’s TV show that, back in the 80s, broke all the rules with its sense of humour, freedom and imagination. So what made La bola so cool for everyone? The answer: CREATIVITY.

Without a doubt, the line that marked me the most from TV at that time:

“Alone you can’t, with friends you can.”



And the one I liked the least (maybe because of my age… I didn’t get it):

“Long live evil, long live capital!”
