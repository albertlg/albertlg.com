Basketball is an art:

The thing is, real artists are not that common...

A parable – an anecdote:
If you were walking down the street and came across an empty soda can on the ground, what would you do?
When I was a kid, they told me that a basketball player would pick it up with their hands, try to dribble it, and then “sink” it into the first trash can they found… instead of kicking it, like most people would.
