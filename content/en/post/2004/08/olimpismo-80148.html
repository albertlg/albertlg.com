The different nations of the world should not be measured at the Olympic Games ONLY by the medals they win. This ranking currently carries an importance that, in my view, does not truly align with the Olympic spirit.
It is part of it, but it is not the whole.

There are many other extremely important values, such as sportsmanship, team spirit (in the case of team sports), humility, self-improvement, competitiveness, awareness of one’s own limitations, adaptability to circumstances… which receive far less media attention.

Obviously, this does not diminish the importance of efficiency and results, which in this case are none other than the desired medals. Although there are victories that receive no reward. ;)
